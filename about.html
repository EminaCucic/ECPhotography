About Me

Born in Doboj, Bosnia and Herzegovina, living in Hardegsen (Germany), and in love with photography, nature, traveling, books, and languages.

I grew up in a small village surrounded by gorgeous scenery and breathtaking views that inspired me to take my first photos.

My passion for languages inspired me to study the English language and literature. Besides English, I speak German, Spanish, Bosnian, Croatian, and Serbian. I am currently learning various computer programming languages that helped me code this website :)

Photography is like a silent language that I use to tell the story of the moments that caught my attention.

There is something special in observing things through the lens. But capturing moments that will always be available in our photo library and the impact those photos have on us is pure magic. That's all it is!

- Emina Cucic
